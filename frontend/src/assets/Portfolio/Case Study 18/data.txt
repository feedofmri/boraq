Overview
SOHOJOGI is a one-stop household service platform designed to make daily life easier by connecting users with trusted and verified professionals such as plumbers, electricians, cleaners, mechanics, and appliance repair experts. With a secure and user-friendly system, SOHOJOGI eliminates the hassle of finding reliable help by offering location-based service matching, a seamless booking process, real-time updates, and a clean, responsive interface. It addresses common challenges such as trust issues, inconsistent service quality, and unverified credentials, ensuring both users and service providers benefit from a reliable and transparent ecosystem. By focusing on trust, security, and convenience, SOHOJOGI stands out as the go-to solution for homeowners and skilled professionals alike, bridging the gap between household needs and quality service delivery.

Challenges and Objectives
As the developer of SOHOJOGI, my primary challenges lie in ensuring cross-platform performance and scalability, integrating secure and reliable APIs for booking and payments, implementing strong authentication and data protection, and delivering real-time features such as live booking updates and notifications. I also face the responsibility of maintaining a consistent user experience across Android, iOS, and web, while managing a robust backend that can handle growing volumes of users and service providers. My objectives are to build a clean and scalable architecture, optimize app performance for speed and reliability, and create a seamless, user-friendly interface that enhances trust and convenience. Additionally, I aim to uphold best practices in code quality, security, and testing, while continuously improving the app through monitoring, updates, and the expansion of features to meet evolving user needs.
