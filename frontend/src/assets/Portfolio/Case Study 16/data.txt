Overview
Dhaka Bakery Sales Management is an innovative platform developed to revolutionize how bakeries manage sales, inventory, and customer interactions. With growing digital demands in the F&B sector, the objective was to build a comprehensive tool that simplifies daily operational tasks while providing actionable insights.




Challenges and Objectives
Operational Efficiency: The design needed to simplify complex sales and inventory management tasks.
User Engagement: Catering to staff with varying digital literacy levels required an accessible and straightforward interface.
Data Visualization: Presenting real-time sales trends and inventory levels in a visually digestible format.
The Design Process
Discovery Phase:
Collaborated with bakery owners and managers to understand the intricacies of daily operations.
Mapped out the traditional workflows, identifying bottlenecks and potential areas for digital optimization.
Conceptualization:
Defined clear personas – from the inventory manager to the front-of-house staff.
Brainstormed features that integrated inventory tracking, sales logging, and customer management into a singular dashboard.
Wireframing & Iterative Prototyping:
Developed initial sketches and low-fidelity wireframes to validate core functionality.
Transitioned to high-fidelity prototypes with interactive elements that allowed real-time simulation of daily tasks.
UI Design & Branding:
Employed a warm, inviting color palette that resonated with the bakery’s brand.
Designed distinct modules for sales, inventory, and analytics to streamline navigation and ease user comprehension.
User Testing and Final Tuning:
Conducted user testing sessions within operational hours to capture real-time user feedback.
Refined interactions and improved visual consistency, ensuring that the final product was robust and user-friendly.
Final Outcome
The revamped sales management system offers a seamless digital solution for day-to-day bakery operations. The intuitive interface has notably decreased training time and operational errors while increasing overall efficiency. Users now have immediate access to critical data, enabling prompt decision-making and enhanced customer service.

Key Takeaways
Digital transformation can optimize traditional business processes.
Combining clear visual hierarchies with user-friendly interactions drives operational efficiency.
Iterative testing with real users yields a design that genuinely meets business needs.
