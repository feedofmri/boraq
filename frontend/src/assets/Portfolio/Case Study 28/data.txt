Case Study #28: EZS – iPhone 16 Pro Max Accessory Campaign
Leave a Comment / Case Study, UI & Branding / By Boraq
Overview
With the launch of Apple’s iPhone 16 series, EZS, a tech accessories brand, aimed to create a modern visual campaign that highlights compatibility, premium design, and product quality.
This project showcases how thoughtful design, lighting, and layout can elevate everyday tech accessories — turning chargers, cables, and cases into part of a premium lifestyle experience.

The Challenge
EZS wanted to communicate three key messages:

Compatibility clarity — users needed to instantly recognize which iPhone 16 models supported EZS accessories.
Premium look & feel — visuals had to match the minimal Apple aesthetic while standing out as a separate brand.
Visual storytelling — display the accessories as elegant tech essentials rather than ordinary gadgets.
The design challenge was to balance informational clarity with high-end brand presentation.



Outcome
The final campaign successfully positioned EZS accessories as premium and trustworthy Apple-compatible products.
By using elegant composition and visual clarity, the design achieved:

Instant product understanding through compatibility cues.
Higher engagement on social media due to its clean, high-end aesthetic.
Brand recognition boost by aligning EZS’s look with modern consumer tech expectations.
