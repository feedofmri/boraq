Case Study #33: AUST CSE Carnival 6.0 – Reimagining the Tech Fest Experience
Leave a Comment / Case Study, UI & Branding / By Boraq
Overview
AUST CSE Carnival 6.0, presented by Havit and organized by AUST CSE Society, is one of the largest inter-university tech festivals in Bangladesh.
The 2025 edition united thousands of students, innovators, and developers under one banner — celebrating creativity, coding, and technology.

Boraq was entrusted with crafting the complete brand and design system — from visual identity and promotional materials to event collaterals and digital experience — ensuring a consistent, modern, and energetic look for all touchpoints.

The Challenge
The challenge was to refresh the CSE Carnival brand while maintaining its academic prestige and youthful energy.

The design system needed to:

Represent innovation, logic, and energy in a cohesive visual language.
Be adaptable across multiple sub-events (Hackathon, Programming Contest, Quiz, Math Olympiad, UI/UX Competition, and more).
Ensure clear sponsor visibility and a premium feel across print, merchandise, and digital platforms.
Create a tech-forward yet welcoming aesthetic that resonates with both participants and corporate partners.













The Solution
We designed a unified event identity that visually communicated energy, innovation, and competition.

Core Visual Direction
Color Palette:
Dark Emerald (#002E2E) for sophistication and depth.
Electric Green (#80FF6E) and Lime Gradient for energy and visibility.
Subtle gradients and light bursts for futuristic motion.
Typography:
Bold geometric sans-serifs for modern readability.
Monospaced and italic highlights referencing tech and code aesthetics.
Motif & Grid System:
Inspired by circuit paths, vector waves, and dynamic motion lines — representing data flow, logic, and digital creativity.
Event Collateral Design
👕 Official Polo T-Shirt
Minimal design featuring title sponsors and event logos.
Corporate-gray tone with embroidered logos for a premium appearance.
📰 Promotional Posters & Leaflets
Clean grid layout balancing tech textures and sponsor space.
QR-based registration posters promoting digital convenience.
🌐 Website Launch Campaign
Coordinated promotional banners announcing the digital platform launch.
Consistent interface visuals aligned with Boraq’s event site design.
🏆 Prizepool Announcement
Highlighted “60K+” typography with gradient lighting and confetti motion cues.
Built for both print and digital reach.
Design Impact
The 6.0 edition branding elevated AUST CSE Carnival into a digitally cohesive, high-energy brand ecosystem, setting a new standard for student tech events in Bangladesh.
