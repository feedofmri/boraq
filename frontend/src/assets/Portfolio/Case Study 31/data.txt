Case Study #31: প্রারম্ভ প্রয়াস ২৪ (Prarombho Proyas 24) – AUST Civilian’s Identity & Event Collateral Design
Leave a Comment / Case Study, UI & Branding / By Boraq
Overview
প্রারম্ভ প্রয়াস ২৪ (Prarombho Proyas 24) was the flagship annual event organized by AUST Civilian’s Club, celebrating innovation, engineering spirit, and creative collaboration among civil engineering students.
To match the grandeur and energy of the event, a cohesive brand identity and merchandise system was designed — connecting the essence of construction, creativity, and community through a modern visual language.

The Challenge
The event aimed to represent civil engineering’s precision and creativity while remaining approachable and youthful for students and visitors.
The design system needed to:

Establish a consistent visual identity across all event materials.
Balance technical structure (architecture & engineering themes) with vibrant energy (student engagement & culture).
Deliver print-ready designs for production items like ID cards, pens, and keyrings.
Create a memorable physical experience for attendees through branding touchpoints such as gate banners and souvenirs.
The challenge was to merge academic professionalism with event enthusiasm.





The Solution
A unified event identity was built around a blue & neon-green theme, reflecting both stability (engineering) and vitality (student life).
Each item in the collateral system followed the same branding structure — minimal yet expressive, visually connecting all parts of the event.

Core Visual Elements
Logo Composition: The skyline-style event logo represents infrastructure, teamwork, and progress — the backbone of civil engineering.
Color Palette:
Deep Navy Blue (#0E1C3A) — symbolizes professionalism, structure, and technical depth.
Bright Cyan & Neon Green Accents — symbolize creativity, optimism, and student energy.
Typography: Modern geometric fonts for high legibility and brand consistency across print and digital mediums.
Graphic Motifs: Construction silhouettes, grid lines, and geometric forms inspired by architectural blueprints.
Key Deliverables
🪪 Event ID Card
Designed for organizers and participants.
Features the event’s skyline logo with subtle gradient accents.
Includes clean typographic hierarchy and reinforced lanyard branding.
🚪 Entrance Gate Design
Conceptualized as the visual centerpiece of the venue.
Integrated light effects, abstract human figures, and city skyline graphics for an immersive welcome experience.
🔑 Premium Key Ring
Compact and durable design reflecting the event logo.
Double-sided print with matte finish — both collectible and functional.
🖊️ Premium Pen Packaging
Minimal design using eco-friendly brown kraft paper sleeve.
Printed logo and tagline in green — blending sustainability with elegance.
Each piece was thoughtfully designed to align with the event’s central theme:

“Building Beyond Boundaries.”

Impact
The new event branding created a cohesive and premium experience across all materials — from attendee entry to take-home souvenirs.
Key achievements include:

Strong visual identity consistency across 8+ event touchpoints.
Increased student participation and recognition through unified branding.
Successful print production and sponsor visibility due to color and layout precision.
The event’s visual communication elevated AUST Civilian’s image as a professionally managed, design-forward student organization.
