Overview
Sohojogi is a comprehensive mobile application designed to simplify everyday tasks and enhance user productivity. Focusing on an engaging and intuitive user experience, the project aimed to integrate various functionalities into a single, user-friendly platform that caters to modern-day needs.




Challenges and Objectives
Multi-Functionality: The app needed to provide diverse services without overwhelming the user.
Intuitive Navigation: Ensuring that users could seamlessly transition between tasks.
Responsive Design: Maintaining consistency and performance across a range of devices, including smartphones and tablets.
The Design Process
User Research & Ideation:
Conducted surveys and focus group discussions to gather insights on daily challenges and desired functionalities.
Developed detailed personas and user stories that guided the design process.
Information Architecture & Flow Mapping:
Structured the app’s content hierarchy to ensure a logical and intuitive user journey.
Defined interaction flows that minimized the number of steps to perform key functions.
Wireframes, Prototyping, and Iteration:
Started with sketches and low-fidelity wireframes to layout core functionalities.
Progressed to high-fidelity interactive prototypes, integrating feedback from stakeholder reviews and user testing.
Visual and Interaction Design:
Emphasized a clean and modern aesthetic with consistent color schemes and typography.
Developed smooth animations and transitions to enhance overall user engagement.
Testing & Optimization:
Employed both in-house testing and beta user groups to identify usability issues.
Optimized the app’s performance, ensuring smooth transitions and quick load times, even on older devices.
Final Outcome
The Sohojogi app successfully merges utility with elegance, providing a platform that enhances daily routines while maintaining a high level of usability. Its refined design and seamless transitions have improved user engagement and satisfaction, setting a new standard for multi-service mobile applications.

Key Takeaways
A unified interface can effectively balance multiple functionalities.
Prototyping and user feedback are crucial in fine-tuning complex apps.
Modern UI elements such as smooth animations enhance user engagement and overall satisfaction.
