Case Study #29: Vastra – Crafting a Reliable and Elegant Brand Identity
Leave a Comment / Case Study, UI & Branding / By Boraq
Overview
Vastra is a textile and apparel brand that stands for trust, reliability, and refined craftsmanship.
The brand required a strong, elegant, and memorable visual identity that could represent both its industrial presence and aesthetic appeal.

The resulting identity system — including the logo, color palette, typography, and stationery — conveys professional confidence and premium quality, embodying the brand’s tagline:

“A Thing of Reliability.”

The Challenge
In a competitive apparel market, Vastra needed a brand identity that:

Communicates reliability and trust without losing modern appeal.
Works consistently across print and digital media.
Balances strength (industrial textiles) with elegance (fashion-forward vision).
Establishes visual cohesion through logo, color, and typography.
The goal was to build a professional and timeless identity that resonates with both B2B clients and end consumers.




The Solution
We created a bold yet refined identity system centered around a distinctive “V” mark symbolizing textile layers, motion, and growth.

Logo Design
The “V” icon is crafted with dynamic wave forms representing fabric folds — a nod to the textile heritage.
Its sharp geometry conveys precision and dependability, while the curved motion adds elegance and flexibility.
The wordmark uses Aldo The Apache, giving the logo a unique character with a sense of stability and modernity.
Color Palette
#582212 – Deep Maroon: Strength, heritage, and warmth.
#332C28 – Charcoal Brown: Professionalism and depth.
#020202 – Black: Authority and timelessness.
Together, they evoke trust, tradition, and modern sophistication — ideal for a brand rooted in textiles.
Typography
Logo Font: Aldo The Apache — for a bold, industrial character.
Body Font: Poppins — clean and contemporary, ensuring readability across all mediums.
Outcome
The new Vastra identity:

Projects trust and craftsmanship — aligning with the tagline “A Thing of Reliability.”
Establishes a distinctive visual presence adaptable across media.
Creates an immediate impression of professionalism and quality, boosting the brand’s market visibility.
