Client Overview
Boraq Space, a side business of Boraq.dev, is a Bangladeshi-based eCommerce platform dedicated to selling high-quality tech products. The primary aim was to create a seamless shopping experience for tech enthusiasts within Bangladesh while maintaining modern design aesthetics and robust performance.

Project Goals
Develop a fully functional eCommerce website tailored to tech product sales.
Ensure mobile responsiveness and user-friendly navigation.
Integrate essential features like secure payment gateways, a wishlist system, and dynamic product filtering.
Optimize for SEO to improve online visibility.
Build a scalable structure to accommodate future business growth.
Challenges
Limited Target Audience: The website caters exclusively to Bangladeshi customers, requiring localized design and content strategies.
Wide Range of Products: Managing different categories like wearables and accessories and computing products required meticulous categorization and inventory management.
Secure Payments: Ensuring customer trust through safe payment solutions.
Our Approach
Requirement Analysis:
We conducted multiple sessions with Boraq.dev to understand their goals, target audience, and preferred features.
Technology Stack:
CMS: WordPress with WooCommerce for eCommerce functionality.
Theme: Custom-designed theme to ensure a unique brand identity.
Plugins: Integration of essential tools like payment gateways, SEO, and product filters.
Design and Development:
A sleek and minimalist design reflecting the tech-oriented focus of Boraq Space.
Responsive UI for optimal performance on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
Clear categorization of products and intuitive navigation for an effortless shopping experience.
Testing and Optimization:
Conducted extensive usability tests to identify and resolve potential issues.
Optimized website loading speed to enhance user experience.
Deployment and Support:
Launched the website successfully within the stipulated timeline.
Provided a detailed user guide for site management and ongoing maintenance support.
Key Features Delivered
Mobile-responsive design.
Wishlist and cart functionality.
Secure payment gateways.
SEO optimization for search visibility.
Heatmap integration to track user behavior.
Results
Successfully launched a user-friendly eCommerce platform that met all client requirements.
Increased user engagement and positive customer feedback within the first month of launch.
Scalable platform, ready to accommodate future expansion and advanced features.
Client Testimonial
“Boraq.dev exceeded our expectations! The Boraq Space eCommerce platform is seamless, user-friendly, and perfectly tailored to our needs. We’re thrilled with the results and look forward to growing our business with this robust foundation.”
