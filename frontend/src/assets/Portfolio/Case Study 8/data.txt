Our team had the incredible opportunity to contribute to AUST CSE Carnival 3.0 through creative graphics design. This week-long event brought the AUST community together to commemorate the legacy of Ayon Raihan Bhai, honoring his passion for technology and innovation. The carnival featured engaging activities, fostering collaboration, learning, and inspiration among students, alumni, and tech enthusiasts.

Here’s a glimpse of what this vibrant event offered:












Event Highlights
Intra AUST Programming Contest (IAPC): A thrilling 5-hour competition where AUST students showcased their coding skills.
CodeClearance (Code Refactoring Contest): Participants elevated unclean code into refined, efficient solutions.
Interface Elysium (UI/UX Competition): Teams crafted innovative app designs using tools like Figma and Adobe XD.
DevNest Expo (Software Exhibition): A showcase of diverse software projects, from apps to games.
SoccerBot Championship (Robo-Soccer): A thrilling bot-powered soccer showdown.
Each segment aimed to inspire creativity, teamwork, and technological advancement, reflecting Ayon Bhai’s vision.

Our Role in the Carnival
To enhance the event experience, our design team developed a cohesive visual identity, including:

Event Banners: Capturing the carnival’s essence with vibrant and dynamic designs.
Social Media Posters: Amplifying engagement and excitement leading up to the event.
Custom Badges and Certificates: Adding a personal touch to participant recognition.
Event Brochures: Delivering a seamless, visually appealing overview of the activities.
Behind the Scenes: The Design Journey
Crafting these materials involved close collaboration with the event organizers to ensure alignment with their vision. Every design choice—from color schemes to typography—was selected to resonate with the event’s purpose and audience.

A Lasting Impact
The event successfully celebrated the life and contributions of Ayon Raihan Bhai, inspiring participants to embrace innovation and push the boundaries of technology. We are proud to have been part of this meaningful initiative.
