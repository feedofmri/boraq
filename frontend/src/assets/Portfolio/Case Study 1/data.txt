Case Study #1: Proshno – Community Knowledge Sharing Platform
Leave a Comment / Case Study, Web & App / By feedofmri
Introduction
Proshno is a knowledge-sharing platform designed to foster community interactions by allowing users to ask questions, give answers, and engage in topic-based groups. Built on WordPress, it caters to students, professionals, and the general public, aiming to create a valuable resource for information exchange. Visit the official Proshno website to explore.

Project Background
Proshno was conceived to offer an online space where users can both seek advice and share knowledge. With a simple, user-friendly interface, it empowers users to engage in meaningful discussions and build communities around shared interests.

Key Features of the Knowledge Sharing Platform
Home Page: Displays recent and popular questions.
Add Group: Users can create groups around specific interests or subjects.
Groups: Organized discussions allow for focused interactions among users.
Badges: Incentives for active participation, increasing engagement.
Questions & Polls: Core functionality where users can post questions and gather opinions.
Tags: Categorizes topics for easy search and content discovery.
Users & FAQs: Allows user profile management and provides essential onboarding information.

Technical Stack
WordPress powers Proshno, offering flexibility for custom functionalities and a strong foundation for future scalability. WordPress also enables the integration of features such as badges and group interactions, crucial for a knowledge-sharing platform like Proshno.

Design and Development
The knowledge-sharing platform was developed with an emphasis on usability. Starting with wireframes, we aimed for a minimalistic design, ensuring that navigation remains simple and intuitive. The development phase focused on creating essential features like groups, badges, and question-based interactions.

Key Development Process
Planning: Defined the target audience and specific platform features.
Prototyping & Design: Created designs that focus on usability and community engagement.
Development: Customized WordPress to support Proshno’s unique features.
Testing: Conducted usability testing to refine user experience.
Launch: Introduced the platform with initial community-focused content.
Feedback & Iteration: Improved features based on user feedback for better engagement.
Outcome
Proshno successfully launched, gathering positive feedback from users for its ease of use and community focus. With its foundational features, Proshno has the potential to scale, attracting a larger community of knowledge seekers and contributors.

Key Takeaways
Community Engagement is essential for Proshno’s growth.
Scalability: WordPress provides flexibility for feature expansion.
Continuous Improvement: User feedback plays a crucial role in refining Proshno.
