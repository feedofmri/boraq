Case Study #32: Moushum (মৌসুম) – A Fresh Identity Rooted in Nature
Leave a Comment / Case Study, UI & Branding / By Boraq
Overview
Moushum (মৌসুম) — meaning season in Bangla — is a brand inspired by the rhythm of nature, agriculture, and growth.
It represents a harmonious connection between the environment, sustainability, and local culture.
The logo and brand identity were designed to reflect Moushum’s mission — celebrating natural abundance with simplicity, freshness, and authenticity.

The Challenge
The challenge was to design a brand identity that:

Captures the essence of growth and nature while appealing to a modern audience.
Merges Bangladeshi tradition with a contemporary design language.
Works across print and digital platforms, including packaging, social media, and stationery.
Creates a visual system that feels organic, honest, and refreshing.
The goal: Build a timeless visual brand that’s easily recognizable, emotionally warm, and deeply rooted in the values of agriculture and community.




The Solution
We crafted a visual identity that celebrates green energy, harvest, and renewal — inspired by the vibrant Bangladeshi landscape.

Logo Design
The wordmark “মৌসুম” is rendered in modern Bangla typography, rounded for friendliness yet bold for legibility.
A leaf and wheat motif rises from the top, symbolizing life, harvest, and continuous growth.
The smooth letter flow mirrors natural movement — much like seasons changing.
Color Palette
Primary Green (#6ED76E): Growth, freshness, and sustainability.
Deep Forest Green (#2E7A3F): Strength and stability.
Soft Yellow (#F6E67D): Optimism, sunlight, and agricultural roots.
The colors together evoke an ecosystem of balance and vitality.
Typography
Bangla Display Type: Custom stylized for the logo to retain local essence.
Latin Companion Font: Clean sans-serif for English materials and contact details.
Impact
The Moushum identity establishes:

A memorable cultural connection through typography and symbolism.
Versatile usability across print, digital, and social platforms.
A positive emotional resonance with consumers seeking authenticity and eco-conscious values.
The new design successfully positions Moushum as a modern, green-forward brand that honors both heritage and progress.
