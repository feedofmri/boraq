Our design team was thrilled to create a cohesive and engaging visual identity for the Naimul Memorial Cricket League Spring 2023. This cricket tournament, organized in memory of Naimul Bhai, brought the AUST CSE community together for an exhilarating week of sportsmanship and camaraderie. Held at the B.G. Press Playground from December 2nd to 9th, 2023, the event celebrated both skill and unity, with participants from various groups showcasing their cricketing talents.





About the Event
The league was meticulously organized by the AUST CSE Society and powered by Qubits45 and Syntax50. With eight teams competing in two groups, the tournament had dynamic formats for both boys’ and girls’ matches, ensuring inclusivity and excitement. From 8-over group-stage matches to intense 10-over finals, players demonstrated exceptional teamwork and athleticism.

The event followed strict rules, emphasized discipline, and provided an array of trophies and crests for exceptional performances. This created a highly competitive and spirited atmosphere that honored the legacy of Naimul Bhai.

Design Highlights
Our team crafted a range of graphics, including:

Event Posters: Designed to captivate attention and highlight key details about the tournament.
Team Logos and Jerseys: Ensuring each team had a unique identity while maintaining a unified aesthetic.
Social Media Posts: Engaging designs for promotions, updates, and results.
Tournament Rulesheets and Group Tables: Visually appealing and easy-to-understand formats.
Champion and Participant Certificates: A polished and memorable keepsake for players.
Conclusion
The designs not only captured the essence of the event but also amplified its reach and impact. This case study showcases our ability to deliver high-quality graphics that resonate with the spirit of the occasion while effectively communicating event details.
